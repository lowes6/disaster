📖 HOW TO PLAY (MAKEY MAKEY)

The entire game uses ONLY the Arrow Keys ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ and the Spacebar!

🛒 PHASE 1: GEAR SHOPPING

A random disaster is coming! You start with $500.

⬅️ ➡️ ⬆️ ⬇️ Move your selection

SPACE Buy an item (or remove it if in cart)

⬇️ To Bottom Move cursor down to checkout to start game!

🏠 PHASE 2: SETUP YOUR HOME

Now place your gear around the house to prepare!

⬅️ ➡️ ⬆️ ⬇️ Walk around your home

SPACE (near a zone) Place/pickup held item

SPACE (in empty space) Cycle to the next item in your bag!

🌪️ PHASE 3: SURVIVE THE DISASTER!

The disaster automatically strikes after a 30s prep timer!

🛡️ Placed items Reduce damage! More protection = less damage.

HOLD SPACE (1 sec) Consume a healing item you are holding!

🛒 GEAR SHOP

🚨 TORNADO INCOMING!

Wind barriers and storm shutters will help protect your home!
