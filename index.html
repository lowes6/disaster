📖 HOW TO PLAY

Use ONLY Arrow Keys ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ and Spacebar! (Or tap the screen)

🛒 1. SHOP

A random disaster approaches! Start with $500.

⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Move cursor
SPACE Buy or Remove
⬇️ Bottom Go to checkout

🏠 2. SETUP

Place gear in zones (🪟 Windows, 🚪 Door) to protect your home!

⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Walk around
SPACE (near zone) Place/pickup
SPACE (empty area) Cycle bag!

🌪️ 3. SURVIVE

The disaster strikes after 30s! Survive the wave.

🛡️ Placed items reduce damage.
❤️ Health/Home at 0% = Game Over.
HOLD SPACE (1s) Consume healing item!
